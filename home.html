Juice Fact

what was the first fresh juice

The first instance of what could be called fresh juice dates back to 8000 BCE with evidence of grape juice production. However, the first regularly produced and widely known fresh juices were likely lemonade and grape juice, with lemonade originating in the Middle East and appearing in 16th-century Europe, and grape juice being documented as early as 8000 BCE.(Source: wikipedia)
